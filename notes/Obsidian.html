Installing and updating themes

The installation/update procedure is the same, go to Settings > Appearance > Community themes > Browse, look for the theme you want, and press Use.

Customizing themes

Some themes (like Yin and Yang) are integrated into the Style Settings plugin, and some have their own plugin.

Theming Obsidian Publish

The easiest way of theming Obsidian Publish is to simply copy your obsidian theme. Copy the theme css file to your vault and name it publish.css, then publish it using the in-app modal. For the Yin and Yang theme this would be cp <VaultRoot>/.obsidian/themes/Yin\ and\ Yang.css <VaultRoot>/publish.css.
